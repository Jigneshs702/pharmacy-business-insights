# Pharmapresence services (fallback snapshot)

Use this file only when you can't read `src/pages/services/*.astro` directly from the `pharmacy-business-insights` repo (i.e. this skill is being used somewhere else). If the repo is available, prefer reading the live `.astro` files — this snapshot can drift out of date.

## Pharmacy Websites
**Headline:** A website that books patients, not just lists your opening hours.
**Pain points it targets:** Site hasn't changed since it was built; patients can't book online so they ring, and if the phone's busy they're gone; private services buried on one generic "Services" page that ranks for nothing; slow on mobile.
**What it delivers:** A dedicated, search-optimised page per profitable service; a fast modern build; online booking, click-to-call and enquiry forms wired to reminders on every page; the pharmacy owns the asset outright (no rental lock-in).

## Google Business Profile Optimisation
**Headline:** Win the local searches that happen before every booking.
**Pain points it targets:** Profile just says "Pharmacy" while competitors show up for the same private services; stale photos, wrong hours, unanswered reviews; no visibility into how many calls/direction-requests the profile generates or loses; a worse competitor outranks them purely because their profile is maintained.
**What it delivers:** A full audit benchmarked against local competitors; every service properly listed with the search terms patients actually use locally; a review-generation system; ongoing local map-pack ranking for the pharmacy's most profitable services.

## AI Agents
**Headline:** An extra team member who never takes a break — and costs less than one shift a week.
**Pain points it targets:** Phone rings out during the lunch rush and every missed call is a missed booking; staff repeat the same twenty questions daily (prices, hours, "do you do travel jabs?"); patients due a follow-up or repeat appointment quietly never come back; existing AI tools are built for US chains, not UK community pharmacies.
**What it delivers:** An agent trained on the pharmacy's own services, prices, availability and tone; connected to website, WhatsApp and phone, writing bookings straight into the diary; automated follow-ups, reminders, review requests and rebooking prompts; a monthly report of conversations, bookings and revenue generated.

## Bookings Booster
**Headline:** Fill your private clinics with paying patients.
**Pain points it targets:** The clinic room and training are ready but bookings are a trickle; patients ring competitors first because they rank first on Google; enquiries don't convert to bookings because there's no online booking, reminders, or follow-up; staff are paid for clinic hours that sit empty.
**What it delivers:** A map of every step a patient takes from Google search to sitting in the consultation room, showing exactly where they're lost; a fixed Google Business Profile plus locally-ranking service pages; a booking flow completable in under a minute; automated reminders, review generation and seasonal follow-up sequences; clear monthly numbers on searches, calls, bookings and revenue.
